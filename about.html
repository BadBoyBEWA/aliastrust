About Alias Trust Company

Your trusted partner in gold investment and wealth preservation since 2007

Our Story

Alias Trust Company was founded in 2007 with a simple mission: to provide secure, transparent, and accessible gold investment services to individuals and institutions worldwide. Based in Dubai, the heart of the global gold trade, we leverage our strategic location and deep market expertise to offer unparalleled service to our clients.

Our founders recognized the growing need for reliable gold investment solutions in an increasingly uncertain financial landscape. With decades of combined experience in precious metals trading, wealth management, and financial services, they established Alias Trust Company to bridge the gap between traditional banking and modern gold investment needs.

Today, we serve thousands of clients across the globe, managing over $500 million in gold assets with a commitment to security, transparency, and client success.
