Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account?

+

Opening an account is simple. Click the "Open Account" button on our homepage, fill out the registration form, and our team will guide you through the verification process. You can start trading within 24 hours of account approval.

What are the minimum investment requirements?

+

We offer flexible investment options starting from as little as 0.1 oz of gold. There are no minimum balance requirements, and you can invest as much or as little as you're comfortable with.

How secure is my gold storage?

+

Your gold is stored in our state-of-the-art vault facilities in Dubai with 24/7 armed security, biometric access control, and full insurance coverage up to $50 million per client.

What are the trading fees?

+

We charge a competitive 1.0% commission per trade with no hidden fees. Storage fees start at $0.50 per oz per month, with volume discounts available for larger holdings.

Can I withdraw my gold physically?

+

Yes, you can request physical delivery of your gold. We offer secure worldwide shipping with full insurance coverage. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days.

Is my investment insured?

+

Yes, all client assets are fully insured by Lloyd's of London with coverage up to $50 million per client. This includes protection against theft, damage, and other risks.
